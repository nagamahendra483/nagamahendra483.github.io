Objective

The primary goal of Car Rental Management System is to provide temporary transportation for those who need it, such as those who don't own a car, those whose vehicles have been damaged or destroyed and are waiting for repairs or insurance money, or those who are out of town on business.

To reserve a car, the User must first log in. The user can quickly search for and reserve automobiles. The user must supply details for bookings, including Booking Dates and Text Message. All the information about the car is given, along with its features and an overview. Additionally, users can alter their profiles, passwords, and testimonials on the website whenever they wish. Admins have the ability to add or manage car brands, vehicles, bookings, testimonials, pages, and many other things.
